Fair Rating

Truer Google Maps ratings for German businesses.

1 Open a Google Maps business page
2 Click the Reviews tab
3 If any reviews were removed, the adjusted rating appears under the notice

Google's notice covers only the last 365 days. We auto-detect the oldest "X years ago" review on the page and use that as a floor. If no year-level timestamp is visible, we don't multiply.

Lower = harsher adjustment. Changes apply on the next page update.
